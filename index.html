Academic Story

This is my academic story, from high school to now.

  • 2017-2019

    High School

    I was a student of Asia International School in Al-Khobar, Saudi Arabia. In high school, I loved IT. Our school followed the British system, so our IT classes were mainly on Microsoft Office, sprinkled with some web development, Photoshop and animation sections. I was a decent, hardworking student, who took part in many activites. I enjoyed my time there and had a wonderful time overall.

  • 2019-2021

    Prince Mohammad bin Fahd University

    I studied at PMU for a couple of years. The idea was to study there for only a year, but due to COVID, I completed a second year. I was an IT student there and was introduced to many web technolgies there. Some of these include: web development, Java programming, Network Programming etc. I enjoyed my first semester, but due to the lockdown, I was not as excited as i used to be. I needed a change...

  • 2021-Now

    University of Debrecen

    I joined UD in September 2021. I first started with the foundation program, which taught me the basics of Python. I become a Business Informatics student and learned many skills such as: Python programming, Digital Marketing, SAP, Tableau, SAS, Jira, Strategic Management, Accounting, Advanced Spreadsheets etc. Many skills that will translate to jobs in my future. I made many friends and learned how to navigate socially. I also started my first internship here. The most important skill I learned here was independence. Overall, it was a fantastic experience.
